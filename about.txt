SARAVANA PAVILION STAY is a highly-rated hospitality property located in Pallivasal, Munnar, Kerala, India. It is highly regarded by travelers for its serene atmosphere, pristine rooms, and close proximity to natural landmarks like the Attukad Waterfall. [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]  
Overview & Location 

• Property Type: Hillside homestay / resort environment. 
• Full Address: Attukad Waterfall Rd, Pallivasal, Kerala 685565. 
• Contact Number: +91 99955 87444. 
• Setting: Secluded terrain surrounded by lush tea gardens, natural vegetation, and a neighboring waterfall. It sits roughly 7 to 8 kilometers south of main Munnar town. [1, 2, 6, 7, 8]  

Key Amenities & Features 

• Scenic Views: Exceptional, direct visual access to the nearby waterfall and surrounding greenery. 
• Dining: No on-site commercial restaurant, but a private chef prepares fresh, highly praised home-cooked breakfast and dinner. 
• Luggage Assistance: Vehicles cannot drive directly to the front door due to the nature trail terrain, but dedicated staff carry all guest luggage to the rooms. 
• Inclusivity: Certified as an LGBTQ+ friendly establishment. [1, 9, 10, 11, 12]  

Guest Feedback & Ratings 
The property boasts a strong 4.7 out of 5 stars rating from guests on Google Maps. 

• The Good: Travelers frequently highlight the "peaceful" and "mind-calming" atmosphere, great hospitality from management (often mentioning executive Anbu), and immaculate room cleanliness. 
• The Vibe: Highly recommended for group vacations, families on holiday, or nature lovers looking for a quiet escape. [1, 13, 14, 15, 16]
